Mac n Cheese

Description:

This isn't your mother's Kraft box mac and cheese! This is old fashioned oven baked gooey cheesy goodness! After making this recipe, you'll be able to tell everyone you know how to make a roux!

Prep time is 20 minutes active but and 50 minutes cook time. Makes 8 servings.

Ingredients:

  • 1 box Rotelle (or whatever your favorite shaped pasta is)
  • 1 /2 stick butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup flour
  • 3 cups cheddar cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook pasta to taste and drain
  2. In the same pot you cooked the pasta, melt the butter in the sauce pan
  3. Mix flour into butter
  4. Slowly mix in milk and simmer until it thickens
  5. Add in Cheese
  6. Mix in Pasta
  7. Bake in Over at 425 for 30 minutes
