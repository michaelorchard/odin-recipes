Overnight Oats

Description:

Overnight Oats is a hearty snack that can even serve as a breakfast. Oats do not require heat to be safe for eating so with overnight oats, you'll let them soak in the fridge to soften up.

Prep time is 5 minutes active but they'll need to be in the fridge for 6 to 8 hours. Suggested serving size is half a cup of oats so make as many as you want (or as many mason jars as you have).

Ingredients:

  • 1 / 2 cup traditional Oats
  • 1 / 3 cup of milk
  • 1 tablespoon of Vanilla syrup

Steps:

  1. Put the oats into the mason jar
  2. Mix the milk and vanilla syrup together (let one of your mason jars serve as the mixing bowl)
  3. Pour milk and syrup over the oats
  4. Seal the jar and leave it in the fridge overnight
